About Stars of Pride

Supporting Asian LGBTQ+

Stars of Pride is a festival that brings awareness to the Asian LGBTQ+ community. Homosexuality and being different from “normal” people has always been hard to accept. And we are here to change. Change that “normal” to be everyone.

We as a community accept everyone no matter the gender, no matter how you are identifies as. Everyone has the right to be who they want to be, marry who they want to love, no matter their gender. Homosexuality is not an illness. It is the modern age where our mindset should be open to all.

Our goal is to not only raise awareness but to help guide those who struggle with their identity. We have many guest speakers and partners that are here to give their experience and to let you know that you are not alone, that you are “normal.”

Our Three Partnered Organizations

The Prism Foundations mission is to empower Asian and Pacific Islander LGBTQIA+ community by rainins funds and using the resouces to build a more just and equitable society. They provides scholorships and grants for students and organization while hosting events of their own to advocate.

ILGA Asia represents organizations for the LGBTI community specifically in Asia with over 100 member organizations. Their goal is to advocate Asia as a safe place for all with freedom and equality. They strive to give representation where diversity is respected.

The National Queer Asian Pacific Islander Alliance was created to develop collaborative projects as early as the 2010. NQAPI supports groups and promote activism and advocates at a national level.
